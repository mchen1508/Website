Michael Chen

Hi, my name is Michael Chen and I like discovering how the world works. During my undergraduate years I was lucky enough to study at the excellent Webb Institute of Naval Architecture and Marine Engineering. Through my time there, I was able to learn extensively about engineering and gained real-life experience in shipyards, yachts/containerships, and in design firms. This really gave me insight into how things are made and the amazing things that are possible when a group of people work together.
      Wanting to learn even more, I decided to obtain my master's degree in Mechanical Engineering at Cal Poly Pomona. Leaning in to the school's motto of "Learn by Doing", I greatly enjoyed my work in the designing and programming of various robots. However, my love of discovery has not stopped with the completion of my schooling. I am always finding something new whether its in improvisational comedy, ballroom dancing, or playing the guitar. In addition, I have several projects in the works including a sound-activated robot manipulator.
